About Dylan Stratton

Dylan Stratton is an actor, director, and digital artist from Oak Park, IL and a recent graduate of St. Olaf College.

Acting Experience

Cabaret: Ernst Ludwig The Cherry Orchard: Petya Trofimov Dead Man's Cellphone: Gordon The Glass Menagerie: Tom Wingfield Oh What A Lovely War: Ensemble Antigone: Haemon

Directing Experience

Shades of Grey: Meg Haley Boeing-Boeing: Marc Camoletti The Stranger: Albert Camus Proof: David Auburn

Boeing-Boeing was co-directed with Memo Rodriguez with an English translation by Beverley Cross. The Stranger was an adaptation of the last 10 pages of the book.
